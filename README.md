# discourse-lab
MS HS Discourse Interactive Game COPPA Compliant
Middle and high school students! Stop the Drama. Start the Strategy.
We’ve all been there: The group project is falling apart. Someone isn’t pulling their weight, someone else is taking over, and the tension is rising. Do you snap? Do you quit? Or do you win?
Welcome to Discourse Lab. This isn't just another boring social-emotional assignment. In this game, you aren't just trying to "be nice"; you're trying to master the art of human interaction.
Level up your social XP by mastering five core power moves:
Regulate: Master the "Pause Protocol." Stay cool when everyone else is losing it.
Inquire: Ask the questions that uncover what people actually mean, not just what they’re saying.
Listen: Gather the intel you need to dismantle defensive barriers.
Reason: Cut through the noise and spot the logic gaps in any argument.
Integrate: Synthesize different points of view to create a solution that actually works for everyone.
Why does this matter? Because life is one big group project. Whether it’s negotiating for more freedom at home, dealing with a tough teammate, or standing your ground in a debate, the person who keeps their cool and builds the best argument wins.
